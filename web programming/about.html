What's the Projects?

Birthday Project "Jaehyun Our Stars" are donated projects. Donors are given 2 (two) ways to donate, namely donation package and donation only.

Where to Donate?

We will give donations from the donors of Jaehyun's 2023 Birthday Project to several donation institutions, opharanages, make an advertisement in various SNS platform to celebrate Jaehyun's 26th birthday, and last but not least birthday cafe events. The donation will be channeled in the name of Jung Jaehyun.

Donation Package
